Hello, it's me.

Programmer turned designer

I'm a self-taught designer, but don't let my lack of formal design education fool you. Being an autodidact has shaped me to be proactive, curious, and humble. I've also had exposure to wearing multiple hats, from product management to customer support, during my time at the early stage startup, Remeeting. I'm currently working as a part-time freelancer while traveling the U.S.

Design for others

My pivot into design occurred after failing multiple times to establish traction. Time and time again, I found my colleagues and I fell prey to spending all our time engineering rather than understanding if we were building the right product for the right people.

Student for life

Failure isn't enjoyable, but it's pushed me to grow. And now I strive to place myself in uncomfortable scenarios. When I'm not designing, you can find me trying to make sense of the world and myself through writing, photography, and travel. Oh, I mentioned I'm traveling the U.S., right? I forgot to say. These travels are via #vanlife.
