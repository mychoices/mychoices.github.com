Programmer turned designer

My pivot to design occurred after I wanted to expand the way I explore ideas. I embraced tools which allowed for clearer communication in the product process and broader ideation in a shorter timeframe. Pursuing design also shaped me to stay curious and humble.

Questioning everything

From thinking outside the box to understanding someone's thoughts and feelings, asking intentional questions plays a crucial role in influencing my perceptions. Examining the "what ifs" and "whys" helps me acknowledge and go beyond my assumptions.

Always learning

When I'm not designing, you can find me trying to make sense of the world and myself through photography, journaling, and traveling. I enjoy experiences that push me beyond my comfort zone. Most recently, I lived out of a minivan while traveling the U.S.
